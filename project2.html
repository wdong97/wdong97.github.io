detecting ai-generated music

fully ai-generated tracks are flooding streaming platforms and crowding out human artists. i wanted to see how reliably a model can tell a machine-made song from a human one.

i pulled together a dataset of human and ai-generated audio, extracted spectral and timbral features, and trained a classifier to flag likely ai-origin tracks. i then measured how much its accuracy drops once a song is transcoded or lightly re-mastered, since that's what happens to real uploads.

next steps: test against fresh generations from newer models, and turn the honest "operating range" into something a distributor could actually use to screen uploads.
