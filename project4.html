agent-based models of market behavior

coming from an economics and math background, i've always been curious how big market patterns emerge from lots of small individual decisions. this project builds markets from the bottom up.

i built a sandbox of interacting agents that each follow simple trading rules, then watched how price dynamics, bubbles, and crashes show up on their own out of the interaction. varying the rules shows what pushes the system toward stability or chaos.

next steps: swap the hand-written rules for learned policies and compare the simulated dynamics against real market data.
